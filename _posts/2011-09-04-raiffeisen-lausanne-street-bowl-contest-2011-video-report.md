This is a video report of the Raiffeisen Lausanne Street & Bowl Contest organized by LSBC in Switzerland. The contest was held on September 1-4, 2011, at the Vidy and HS36 skatepark in Lausanne. The video was filmed and edited by Guillaume J. Lauzier and features music by Quantic – Apricot Morning. The results of the contest are included, with winners listed for the PROS, GIRLS, and AMATEURS categories. The video is in 1280720 HD format.

[Raiffeisen Lausanne Street Bowl Contest 2011](https://vimeo.com/29032796) from [Guillaume J. Lauzier](https://vimeo.com/guillaumelauzier) on [Vimeo](https://vimeo.com).

Take a journey to Tokyo through the captivating lens of Guillaume J. Lauzier in "First Nights in Tokyo." Filmed during the winter of 2012, the video showcases the city's exceptional nightlife and breathtaking landscapes, including Shibuya covered in snow, the Tokyo Tower, and Asakusa's incredible temples. This video takes you on a trip to every popular place in Tokyo, including the stylish bar called The Quest in Roppongi, the fashion shopping district of Omotesando and Harajuku, and more. The soundtrack, "Don't Stop" (Autoerotique remix) by The Subs, adds a pulsing energy to the journey. Shot on a Canon 5D MkII, this video is a must-see for anyone who loves the beauty and excitement of Japan.

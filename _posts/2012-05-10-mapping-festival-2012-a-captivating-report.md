In 2012, I had the opportunity to attend the Mapping Festival in Geneva and produced a video report that showcases the festival's highlights. The report was filmed, edited, and animated by me, Guillaume J. Lauzier, and features music by Rory Phillips – Chamber Music (Original Mix).

The report provides a comprehensive overview of the festival, capturing the excitement and energy of the event through a combination live performances, and behind-the-scenes footage. The report also features stunning visuals and animation that highlight the creativity and innovation of the festival's performers and artists.

The report was presented on VJs TV, a new website that I launched at the time. The website is dedicated to showcasing the best videos from VJs and video artists around the world, with over 1000 videos available for streaming online. The website also includes a VJ channel, which streams videos 24/7 without any ads or breaks, providing a non-stop selection of stunning videos.

At the end of the report, viewers are encouraged to visit vjstv.com/submit to submit their own projects for consideration. The website features a simple form that makes it easy for artists to submit their videos, and I am always looking for new and exciting content to feature on the site.

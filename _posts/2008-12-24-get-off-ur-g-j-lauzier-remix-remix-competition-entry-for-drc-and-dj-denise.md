In 2010, I took part in a remix competition for DRC and DJ Denise, and Mizumo Music, competing against hundreds of other participants. I created an Electro House track using Ableton and Logic Pro, using samples provided by the organizers. The title of my remix was "DRC and DJ Denise – Get Off Ur (G.J. Lauzier Remix)"
